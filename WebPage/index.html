COMP 477 Project - RagDoll Dismemberment

Idea: use Unity to simulate dismemberment of a ragdoll

The idea is to use hierarchical modeling to create a ragdoll model, which allows interaction with its joints. When we drag a joint, it causes its parent limb to be stretched. When the amount of stretch exceeds a pre-defined amount (the Snap Scale property of the DragJoint script component attached to parent limbs).

Once a limb is snapped (de-attached) from the body, it will fall onto the ground. We can use the same joint we initially used to drag the object to pick up the limb. This causes the limb(s) to hang from the joint that we are holding. If the limb is moved towards the parent, and once the distance is less than a specific amount (the Attach Distance property of the DragJoint script), it will re-attach to its original location.
